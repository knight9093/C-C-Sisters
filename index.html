About Us

C&C sisters crochet products are handcrafted, not machine-made, and are as beautiful and strong as a family bond. That is why all products are well-hooked and well-yarned to ensure tightness and decorative appeal, resulting in a beautiful and durable product. It all began with their mother, who is also the proprietor, and was founded in Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia in 2023 because the mother is an Overseas Filipino worker. C&C sisters began as a side hustle when their mother became bored at work, but the mother saw an opportunity to make it big by creating crochet in the same way that a mother expresses her love for her children. By her inspiration to her offspring's CHAREMEDEL & CLEFFAIZAH, like an iron forge in fire, mother makes an art by her hands. C&C Sisters Crochet is an independent product that any mother will appreciate.
